Landor

For this project I had to design a a new office building for the graphic design company Lanfor. I based my design off of the Portland location, using the Eco-Modern style that Portland is known for. Huge green walls, a view from an room or desk area and the use of natural materials accomplished this, making it fit right into its Portland location.

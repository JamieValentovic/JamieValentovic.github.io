Sunrise Hills

For the is project I was required to design a house that I ADA accessible, meaning that a person with a disability can function in the house. Doing this required lowering counter heights in the kitchen, using special appliances, modifying the bathroom to have a roll in shower and making sure there is enough room for a wheelchair to turn around in every room of the house.
